About Snappy Laundry

Snappy Laundry has been proudly serving the Maryland Heights community for years. Founded as a small family-owned business, we have grown into a beloved local staple. Our mission is to provide a warm, welcoming environment, offering top-of-the-line laundry services while maintaining personal connections with our customers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do the machines accept?

Our washers and dryers accept coins. There is also a coin dispenser on-site.

Do you offer detergent if I forget mine?

Absolutely! Our vending machines have detergent, fabric softener, and dryer sheets.

Can I leave my clothes unattended?

We recommend staying on-site for the safety and care of your items.

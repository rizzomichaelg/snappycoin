Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do the machines accept?

Our washers and dryers accept coins. We also provide a coin dispenser on-site.

Do you offer detergent if I forget mine?

Absolutely! Our vending machines have detergent, fabric softener, and dryer sheets.

Can I leave my clothes unattended?

While many customers step away briefly, we recommend staying on-site for the safety and care of your items.
